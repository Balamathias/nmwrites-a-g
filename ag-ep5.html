The Abandoned Genius

Episode 5

Nights were becoming more dreadful and risky for me. Although, I had adapted a bit to the environment but hardly did a day pass by that I did never had a bad time.

I developed a feeling that there was something better out there! At least, I could smell technology a bit. I had to live to fulfill my aspirations.

I understood my home, I was moved to make a new move. I could barely sum up how old I had become, maybe ten or thereabout.

________

I drew my plan and concluded on taking my leave for the towns the following day. I sat all night thinking and reminiscing about those forest moments, how I ate trees to grow.

I could see the stars glaring and promising hope, I saw for the last time in the woods the sun set. To my humble birds, we sang our final lyrics. Morning came by quickly, I could see myself being groomed together to commence a life search journey.

I trekked a few miles and had the feeling that I was either going denser or maybe worse. Although quite nervous about any beastly encounter, I walked stealthily. In order to increase the precision of my journey, I traced the position of the sun by and by.

_____

After a few more miles, I was welcomed by a civilization that lacked quietness as opposed to mine. And a flash through my mind recalled the moments of buzzing flies and chirping birds.  Well, I was seeing a total opposite.

I  made myself comfortable on an extremely low cliff by the tarred road. After taking a few moments of relaxation, I watched some funny scenes on display... How two tired wheels moved from place to place with people without slipping off.

I wondered to some extent how possible that was... At this point, it was getting darker and I felt a rumble and a thunderstorm in my stomach. I yawned extending my jaws for a few more seconds more than normal.

The realities of the streets had just been laid affront me by fate to conquer! ...Yes! I slept on empty stomach and no one cared about anybody... At this point, I missed where I was coming from.

_____

I was a wasting genius!
