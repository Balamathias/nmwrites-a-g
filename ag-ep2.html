The Abandoned Genius

Episode Two

Luckily enough for me, my mom stealthily whisked her footsteps behind them.

She could target the exact place I was laid to die of starvation or probably to be devoured by wild beasts.

It was dawn already, I could feel the gentle ruffle of her cold hands.

She took me from the earth and embraced me. She wept and prayed me to survival.

However, at this point, mom was up to risking her own life as the preceding consequence of her action appeared almost unbearable.

I was only 15 days old when she hid me in a solitary cave.

After a few seconds, she could hear the quakes of a violent marching beast. She laid me in the cave, shrouded me with bullrushes and fled from being devoured.

Mom also narrated her ordeals to Dad who insisted to let go of their misfortune.

He referred to me as a "misfortune" but mom wouldn't listen.

_______

Time fled like eagles, days became months and months never spared becoming years.

Mom kept on visiting and nursing me in the cave for five whole years!

As fate could have it, I was seen by some of the renowned hunters of the time.

When they sighted me from a distance, fear gripped them and they almost took to their heels.

One of the bravest drew closer and closer to me while the rest watched from a distance.

When they sensed that I wasn't violent, they all drew near.

They asked what my name was and how I ended up here but my blue eyes spoke sense into their sanity.

Just about at the same time, mom appeared with some food and water, as she always did. This time, they both came with Dad as he agreed to support her.

It was a clash event, the hunters pointed their guns at them and they surrendered.

"Oh, you! You had the guts to hide this monster child!" One pointed.

"Please don't hurt him!" Mom screamed in tears.

"Seize them!" The Chief hunter ordered.

"We are taking them to the king!" He added.

"And for the boy, the king will know what to do with him." He wrapped up.

_______

At the palace, the king's dome, the king was so much aggressed towards what mom and Dad had done.

He ruled out that mom and Dad be executed.

The elders took counsel on this and agreed with the King.

Daddy pleaded for mercy, he added that it'd be fine if they are exiled and never to return to the land.

"Not with the child!" The King aggressively chimed in.

The rest of the elders coincided as the King ruled out that if they must live to see the next day, they'd have to leave immediately.

Although, this was a very difficult step for them to take, they were left without choice as they shove off the palace for their belongings.

My Parents became the very first people to be ever banned from the land after the mercy of the King.

I was then rendered to be executed.

"How can we be sure that this wizard is not the one responsible for the death of the King's inlaws?" One of the elders accused.

The rest of them agreed... They accused me of mysteriously murdering those people. This was even enough reason for me to be executed.

I was handed over to five valorous men of the community who took me to a serried monstrous forest.

They also took their locally made ammunition with them to enhance their safety over wild beasts.

As they marched with me through the forest to be executed, they heard strange sounds and steps.

It was a wild beast that had been ruminating about the Forests.

The brave men quaked at it's sight as it sped towards them. They abandoned me and fled for their lives.

The beast took after them. On seeing this, I sped into the denser forest with my short marching steps.

________

In the forest, at twilight, I could view how the sun set in the valleys.

I could not hear the steps of mom. I couldn't feel her warm embrace and her lovely kisses and lullabies.

At this point, a new phase had opened for me where I was to cater for myself!

I was just five!
