The Abandoned Genius

Episode 6

Just as the clouds delayed the emergence of the sun, I was also forced to wake from my nightly sleep by an unceasingly beeping car. I was just lucky to have slept just the right duration without turning a side

Maybe I would not have been able to defeat the unceasing hunger that had ravaged my stomach scene. Just about the same morning, I found my self surrounded by a group of Children staring me like a movie on screen.

Well, they might have felt my alien presence, unable to articulate any known language. I could not stand the quite embarrassing scene so I left the place craving for food and they kept staring till I got to the bend where I was out of sight. My attires couldn't spare their betrayal.

I was on the attires I was five years ago! After moments of searching, I secured a dump where varieties of fruits had been disposed. As a fruit friendly person, I filtered the eatables and left the scene.

I could still sight newer spectators staring in awe! Well, I was not in anyway shy...

****

I walked the ailes of the streets and markets like a bat flying at night. At some point, I could sight Kids fairly my age struggling to make ends meet. I was nonetheless spared by the hunger that had befallen them too. I also felt the need to pause at a location and dream dreams.

The night caught up with me and I was wrapped up in sleep and till the following morning when the soldiers in my stomach intensified their war. At some point, it felt like an aggressive soldier threw a missile at the other but missed the target thereby forcing the missile to land on the walls of my stomach causing so much pain.

I endured it all...

______

After about few days, I could no longer bear it, I was almost passing out. I made series of applications to diverse territories that could hire my tender self to work for them. I was plainly denied every offer... I kept on feeding on dumps until I could speak like the others fluently.

I thought about life, Got myself a new attire from the dump and headed for a sachet water company to relearn life. I bought on credit, made sales and returned the integer part of the money to the factory while I spent the rest on food.

I felt shelter was not very necessary at the point even though the heavens never spared their tears on me. I gave in to sleeping by the corner of stores at night. No atom of fear resided in me.

Overtime, I was able to make more sales than my counterparts. I wondered why this set of people admired me. I thought I was cursed or were they mocking me?
